# Go-to-Market Strategy & Implementation Plan

## Executive Summary

This plan outlines a **6-month go-to-market strategy** to launch the Contract Analyzer API, acquire first 100 customers, and achieve $50K MRR. Focus on content-led growth, strategic partnerships, and product-led acquisition.

---

**Timeline**: 2 weeks
