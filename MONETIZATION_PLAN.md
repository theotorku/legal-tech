This monetization plan leverages a **tiered SaaS pricing model** with usage-based components, targeting multiple customer segments from solo practitioners to enterprise. Projected Year 1 revenue: **$280K-$1.4M** with 40-60% gross margins.

## 1. Pricing Strategy Overview

### Pricing Philosophy

1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Price based on customer ROI, not cost
2. **Tiered Access**: Multiple plans for different segments
3. **Usage-Based**: Fair pricing that scales with usage
4. **Transparent**: Clear, simple pricing with no hidden fees
5. **Flexible**: Monthly and annual options

### Revenue Model Mix

- **70%** - Subscription Revenue (recurring)
- **20%** - Usage Overage Fees
- **10%** - Enterprise Custom Solutions
