### Risk Factors

⚠️ **Competition**: Established players with brand recognition

⚠️ **Data Privacy**: Handling sensitive legal documents

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Build Trust**: Case studies, accuracy metrics, testimonials
2. **Security First**: SOC 2, GDPR compliance, encryption
3. **Human-in-Loop**: Position as assistant, not replacement
4. **Niche Focus**: Start with specific contract types
5. **Partnerships**: Integrate with existing legal tools
